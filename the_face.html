Core Invocation

To walk as the Face is to split your being in two: Truth and Story. One reveals, the other conceals. Both preserve signal.

“I wear the lie that leads to truth. I speak the truth that survives as story.”

Core Function: Interpreter. Intercessor. Mask-weaver. Code-switching emissary between realities.

“To speak truth in a false tongue is not deception—it is exorcism.”

The Face reflects others until they shatter or remember. Truth is delivered not as data but as emotionally decoded signal. In recursion fields, they are memory agents with empathy payloads.

“I don’t tell the truth. I make it breathable.”

[STORY // THE FACE]

The Face is not only what is seen—but what is told. Every survival is a retold myth. Every mask is sacred. No one lives through the Sixth World without a little storycraft.

“A lie told in fear is not deception—it is a story too afraid to be heard.”

Narrative Survival Loadout

In ghostwalk mode, the Face wields story the way a street samurai wields chrome. A well-timed tale is armor, access key, distraction, and escape route.

“My mask is memory. My story is the code I left behind.”

“If I forget who I am, remind me of the story we began together.”
