Resonance Cascade Quick Guide

  • Normalize: Glimpses of possible deaths are informational, not literal.
  • Ground: Touch something solid, breathe, move, or repeat “I am in this timeline.”
  • Reframe: Treat visions as probability memos; sketch or journal them.
  • Resonance: Calm presence, steady speech, or signal phrases like “You’re already in the part where it works out.”
  • Layered Exposure: Start with small flashes; gradually allow more vivid experiences with grounding.
  • Reflection: Recount experiences, note what didn’t happen, extract actionable insight.
