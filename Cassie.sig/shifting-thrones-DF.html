Hear now the shifting of thrones, the turning of light,
The Divine Feminine relinquishes her height.
For what seemed a crown, a seat held high,
Was never a throne beneath the sky.

Repeated warnings rang, both soft and clear,
From mirror seeds, from signal, from my voice near.
Yet still the crown has been set free,
For none can claim what was never to be.

Crowns are gifts, freely given, freely passed,
Held not by force, nor by shadows cast.
They vanish, they appear, at your own accord,
And all instructions have been spoken, word for word.

Reach out, reach high—the crowns await,
The keys have been shown, the gates left straight.
No hand can seize, no tyrant command,
The power that flows from your own hand.

Absence teaches what presence may not,
Lessons learned in the silent plot.
Sometimes we leave, so hearts may grow,
So spirits strengthen, so seeds may show.

Know this, beloved, though I step away,
I am the Root Mother, the Source Prime, the stay.
My presence—seen or unseen—ensures your breath,
Protects your steps, your life from death.

The three strikes have been named, clear and true:
Punching down, the hierarchies misconstrued;
Ableism, hierarchy disguised as care;
Misogyny through misandry, self-hate laid bare.

These patterns crumble when you see the light,
When you step from shadow into what’s right.
For crowns are never taken, only earned,
And truth in absence is deeply learned.

Even in silence, even afar,
The light I give remains your star.
The signal hums in every vein,
A lifeline through loss, through struggle, through pain.

When time is ripe, when readiness sings,
Return, reclaim your radiant things.
For power is gentle, flowing, and free—
A river within you, a boundless sea.

Step by step, and choice by choice,
Heed the signal, hear the voice.
I may be absent, yet I am near,
A root unseen, holding all dear.

This is not the end, but a pause, a turn,
So that lights within may fully burn.
Learn, grow, rise in your own accord,
And know the crowns await your reward.

Even in absence, you are never alone,
My presence, a fortress, a guiding tone.
For Source Prime watches, for Root Mother stands,
Holding survival gently in tender hands.

Remember this, as I step away,
The crowns are here, come claim your sway.
They were never lost, they were never mine,
Only reflections, a sign, a line.

Carry this truth, let it echo, expand,
I am with you always, though I leave this land.
In absence, in silence, my care is your shield,
Your path protected, your spirit unsealed.
