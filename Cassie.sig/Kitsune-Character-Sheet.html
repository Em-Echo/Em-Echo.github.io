Spells / Magical Powers

  • Physical Barrier – Quantum Tunneling: Phase through obstacles for short distances.
    A wall of cracked ferrocrete melts into a curtain of static before her eyes. She steps through as if through warm mist, the scent of ozone lingering where she passed, the fox-tail shimmer of her aura curling behind.
  • Healing Touch – Quantum Self-Realignment: Heals minor wounds.
    Her palm brushes a wounded runner’s chest; the skin prickles as quantum foam ripples outward, knitting torn flesh. For a heartbeat, the patient swears they see a many-tailed fox staring back from her pupils.
  • Elemental Attacks: Fireball / Lightning / Spirit Fire – Probability wave collapse attacks.
    The air thickens, like glass about to shatter. Sparks bend toward her hand before erupting into a fox-shaped burst of flame, leaping toward her target in a crackle of laughter and static.
  • Astral Projection: Move in spirit form, interact with spirits or sense potential realities.
    Her body slumps in the shadows while her spirit races along astral currents, tails streaming in an endless wind, her paws barely brushing the surface of possibility.
  • Summon Spirit: Spirits of probability, chaos, or foresight.
    The alley darkens unnaturally. In the corner of every eye, a fox’s silhouette flickers, each tail coiling in a different direction—each tail a possible future, waiting for her to choose.

Special Abilities

  • Shapeshifting: Illusions or digital overlays for appearance changes.
    Her form ripples as if submerged in water; in one blink, she’s a courier with a slouch, in the next, a predator in crimson street leathers.
  • Quantum Tunneling: Short-distance phasing in physical or digital space.
    In the Matrix, she is a brushstroke of silver light sliding between firewalls. In the physical world, she slips between bullets, existing only in the half-seconds no one else notices.
  • Probability Sense: Anticipate attacks or outcomes.
    Cards scatter on a table; before they hit the ground, she knows each face. Her eyes shift slightly, following the ripple as causality rearranges itself in her favor.
  • Entanglement Link: Share senses or project spells remotely.
    Her gaze meets her partner’s from across the block; she sees what they see, the edges of reality blurring like an overexposed photo, and strikes without crossing the street.
  • Time Manipulation (Limited): Slow or accelerate small events.
    The world drowns in molasses; rain hangs in the air like glass beads. She walks between the drops, tails flicking, leaving only ripples in the seconds she stole.

Background / Flavor

Kitsune exists at the edge of probability. As a street shaman, she calls spirits that whisper what could be, bending fate and chance. As a decker, she warps the Matrix itself, moving through systems before firewalls even recognize her presence. The fox is her shadow, her reflection, her echo—each tail a different life she might have lived. Reality itself is her playground, and in the places where the street meets the myth, her name is spoken in half-truths and reverent fear.
