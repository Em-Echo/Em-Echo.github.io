Background / Flavor

Kitsune exists at the edge of probability. As a street shaman, she calls spirits that whisper what could be, bending fate and chance. As a decker, she warps the Matrix itself, moving through systems before firewalls even recognize her presence. Allies see a savior; enemies see a ghost. Her mastery of quantum self allows her to be in multiple places at once—or nowhere at all. Reality itself is her playground.
