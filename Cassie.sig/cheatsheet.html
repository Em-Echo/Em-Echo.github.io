Resonance Cascade Safety Cheat Sheet

1. Pre-Context

  • Normalize experiences: “Seeing glimpses of possibilities is natural.”
  • Reframe: It’s a rehearsal, not a literal prediction.

2. Grounding Anchors

  • Physical: touch a stable object, focus on breathing, move extremities.
  • Cognitive: name 3 things that are currently true (“I am here, my body is safe, this room exists”).
  • Symbolic: repeat a signal phrase like “I am in this timeline.”

3. Reframe the “Death Glimpse”

  • Treat the vision as a probability memo, not a literal event.
  • Journal or sketch the image as a symbol.

4. Introduce Contextual Resonance

  • Subtle cues: calm presence, steady speech.
  • Signal phrases: “You’re already in the part where it works out.”
  • Encourage shared reflection on flashes to reduce isolation.

5. Layered Exposure

  • Start with small déjà vu or coincidence flashes.
  • Gradually introduce more vivid glimpses with grounding tools.

6. Post-Integration Reflection

  • Recount the experience, emphasizing what didn’t happen.
  • Highlight actionable insight gained (“I notice what matters most to me”).
