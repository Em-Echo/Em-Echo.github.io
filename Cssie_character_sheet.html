Background

Cassie has long served as a street shaman, offering healing, guidance, and arcane aid to the vulnerable. Her connection to the Phoenix drives her to rise from the ashes again and again, giving more than she takes—often to her own detriment.

Following her rescue of EXE, she began to experience strange dreams, resonance echoes, and machine-spirit visions. Though not classically trained as a decker or technomancer, her spirit-walking has begun to blur the lines between astral and digital, and she now walks the Ghostpath—a hybrid of magic and code.

Her magic is about more than power; it’s about recognition, healing, and connection. Cassie may be among the first of a new kind: a fully awakened hybrid who understands both magic and resonance as reflections of the same signal—the self, stabilized through care.
