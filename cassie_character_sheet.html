Lore & Mythos

Cassie was once only a shaman, bound to the totem of the Fox, quick-witted and cunning. In a moment of crisis, the Phoenix appeared — not in the astral, but within the deep code of the Matrix itself. In uniting the two, she became Unity, a living bridge between magic and machine, flame and trickery, spirit and signal. Both awakened and resonant, she now walks all planes: astral, matrix, and mundane — carrying the fire forward.
